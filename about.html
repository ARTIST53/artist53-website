About ARTIST53

Built through years of drawing, learning and doing the work.

I am Justin Anderson, the artist and designer behind ARTIST53. My work moves between logo design, illustration, character development, publishing, apparel and brand graphics. The common thread is simple. I like turning ideas into something people can see, feel and remember.

How it started

I began with drawing. Before websites, brand systems and digital tools, there was pencil, paper and the need to make an idea real. That foundation still shapes the way I work today.

Over the years I have created logos for businesses, published children's book illustrations, character systems, album artwork, apparel graphics, marketing pieces and real world brand applications.

ARTIST53 is not about making noise for the sake of it.

It is about making work that has a reason to exist.
