About ARTIST53

Built through years of drawing, learning and doing the work.

I am Justin Anderson, the artist and designer behind ARTIST53. My work moves between logo design, illustration, character development, publishing, apparel and brand graphics. The common thread is simple. I turn ideas into visuals people can recognize, remember and use.

What I bring to the table

I combine drawing ability, design thinking and real production experience. That means I can develop the idea, create the visual language and help carry it into print, merchandise, publishing and real world applications.

Let us build something memorable

Every project begins with a conversation about what the idea needs to communicate and where it needs to live. From there, the goal is to create work with personality, clarity and staying power.
