Sticky Paws

Born from one conversation.

Rob Strickland wanted a character capable of reaching young people with lessons about honesty, integrity and the virtues needed to become a noble adult. During a meeting inside Barnes & Noble, the spark of Sticky Paws was born in less than thirty minutes.

The idea grew into a complete character universe: a lead mascot, supporting cast, expression sheets, book illustration, web concepts, flyers, stickers, T-shirts and potential follow-up titles.
