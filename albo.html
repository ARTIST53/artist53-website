First Published Illustration Assignment

Albo Finds a Home

Traditional craft. Published result.

Created for author Dennis R. LaPoole, this was my first published illustration job. I developed character scenes and environments through pencil drawing, painted the finished illustrations with traditional watercolor, and enhanced the artwork in Photoshop for print production.

The project established the storytelling, composition and character foundation that continues through my current children's publishing work.
